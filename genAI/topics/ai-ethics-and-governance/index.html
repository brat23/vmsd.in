AI Ethics & Governance

Module 4: Advanced Applications

Responsible Implementation

As you build more powerful AI solutions, it becomes increasingly important to consider the ethical implications and establish clear governance policies.

Key Governance Areas

  • Data Privacy and Security: Ensure that any user data processed by your AI is handled securely and that you have clear policies about how it is used.
  • Transparency: Be transparent with users about when they are interacting with an AI. Disclose the limitations of your AI and provide a way for users to give feedback or appeal an AI's decision.
  • Bias Auditing: Regularly test your AI systems for bias and take steps to mitigate it.
  • Accountability: Establish clear lines of accountability within your organization for the outputs and decisions of your AI systems.
