Accelerating Discovery

In the scientific community, AI is being used to analyze complex datasets and accelerate the pace of discovery in fields from medicine to climate science.

Example: Drug Discovery

Pharmaceutical researchers are using AI to analyze vast databases of scientific literature, clinical trial results, and genomic data. The AI can identify potential relationships between genes, diseases, and chemical compounds that would be impossible for humans to find. This helps researchers identify promising candidates for new drugs much more quickly, dramatically reducing the time and cost of drug discovery.
