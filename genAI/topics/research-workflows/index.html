Building an AI-Assisted Process

To truly leverage AI in research, it's best to integrate it into a structured workflow rather than using it in an ad-hoc manner. A well-designed workflow ensures that you are using the right tool for the right task and maintaining high standards of academic integrity.

A Sample Workflow

  1. Brainstorming & Refinement: Use an LLM to brainstorm initial research ideas and then refine them into specific, answerable questions.
  2. Literature Discovery: Use AI-powered academic search engines (like Semantic Scholar) or prompt an LLM to help you find key authors and papers in your field.
  3. Summarization & Synthesis: Feed key papers into an LLM to get concise summaries. Synthesize findings from multiple papers to build your literature review.
  4. Data Analysis: Use AI tools to analyze your data, whether it's coding qualitative data, running statistical tests on quantitative data, or generating visualizations.
  5. Drafting & Editing: Use the AI as a writing partner to draft sections of your paper, improve clarity, check for grammatical errors, and ensure a consistent tone.
