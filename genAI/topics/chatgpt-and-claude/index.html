ChatGPT & Claude

Module 1: Tools & Platforms

The Leading Conversational AIs

ChatGPT and Claude are two of the most advanced and widely used conversational AI platforms. While both are built on Large Language Models, they have different strengths, philosophies, and features that make them suitable for different types of tasks.

ChatGPT (by OpenAI)

As the platform that brought generative AI into the mainstream, ChatGPT is known for its versatility and powerful capabilities.

  • Strengths: Excellent at creative writing, complex reasoning, and code generation. Its flagship models (like GPT-4) are often at the frontier of AI performance.
  • Ecosystem: Features a rich ecosystem including the GPT Store, where users can find and build custom versions of ChatGPT for specific tasks (Custom GPTs), and advanced data analysis capabilities that allow it to write and execute code to analyze files you upload.
  • Use Cases: Ideal for brainstorming, content creation, programming help, and complex problem-solving.

Claude (by Anthropic)

Claude was developed with a strong emphasis on AI safety, ethics, and producing helpful, harmless responses. This focus influences its behavior and capabilities.

  • Strengths: Excels at handling very long documents and text, thanks to its large context window. It's often preferred for tasks involving summarization, analysis, and question-answering on large bodies of text. Its responses are often described as being more reliable and less prone to generating harmful content.
  • Philosophy: Built using a technique called "Constitutional AI," where the model is trained to adhere to a set of principles (a constitution) to ensure its outputs are safe and aligned with human values.
  • Use Cases: Perfect for legal document analysis, summarizing research papers, editing long manuscripts, and any task requiring a high degree of trust and safety.
