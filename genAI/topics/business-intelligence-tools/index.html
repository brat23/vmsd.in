The Convergence of BI and AI

Traditional Business Intelligence (BI) platforms like Tableau and Power BI are increasingly integrating generative AI features, allowing users to analyze data and create dashboards using natural language.

Natural Language Queries

Instead of dragging and dropping fields to build a chart, users can now simply type a request like, "Show me the year-over-year sales growth by region." The BI tool, powered by an LLM, will interpret the request and generate the appropriate visualization automatically. This makes data analysis accessible to a much broader range of business users who may not be expert data analysts.
