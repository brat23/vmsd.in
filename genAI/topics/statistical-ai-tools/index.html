Module 3: Data Analytics

Your AI Statistician

AI is making it easier than ever to perform sophisticated statistical analysis. You can ask an LLM to conduct tests, interpret results, and explain complex statistical concepts in simple terms.

Key Capabilities

  • Running Tests: You can ask an AI to write the code to perform various statistical tests, such as t-tests, ANOVAs, or chi-squared tests, on your data.
  • Interpreting p-values: An AI can explain what the p-value and other results from a statistical test mean in the context of your specific research question.
  • Choosing the Right Test: If you are unsure which statistical test is appropriate for your data and hypothesis, you can describe your situation to an LLM, and it can recommend the correct one to use.

While AI is a powerful assistant, it's still crucial to have a foundational understanding of statistics to ensure you are interpreting the results correctly and not drawing misleading conclusions.
