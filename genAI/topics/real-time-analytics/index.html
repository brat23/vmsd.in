Insights on the Fly

Real-time analytics involves processing and analyzing data as it is generated. This is critical for applications like monitoring website traffic, tracking financial markets, or managing logistics. AI can play a key role in interpreting these fast-moving streams of data.

AI in Streaming Data

An AI model can be used to monitor a real-time data stream and provide alerts or summaries. For example, it could monitor a social media feed and identify emerging viral trends, or watch a stream of server logs and flag potential security threats as they happen.
