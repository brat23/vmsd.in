Visual Storytelling

Module 3: Data Visualization

More Than Just a Chart

Visual storytelling is the art of weaving data, visualizations, and narrative into a compelling story that persuades an audience. An AI can help you structure this narrative and find the story in your data.

Finding the Narrative

After you've created a set of charts, you can present them to an LLM and ask, "What is the story here?" The AI will analyze the visualizations and synthesize them into a written narrative, explaining how the different data points connect and what conclusions they support. It can help you structure your presentation, from the initial hook to the final call to action.
