Module 4: Innovation Methodologies

Changing the Game

Disruptive innovation creates a new market and value network, eventually displacing established market-leading firms, products, and alliances. Generative AI is a powerful engine for creating disruptive applications.

Identifying Opportunities

Disruptive opportunities often arise from using new technology to make a product or service more accessible and affordable. You can use AI to identify these opportunities by asking questions like:

  • "What industries are currently characterized by high costs, complex processes, and limited access?"
  • "How could a generative AI tool simplify the core task in the legal/financial/healthcare industry?"
  • "What service currently available only to large enterprises could be made available to small businesses using AI?"
