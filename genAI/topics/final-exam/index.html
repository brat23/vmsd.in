Module 6: Final Assessment & Certification

Test Your Knowledge

The final exam is a comprehensive test covering all the key concepts from Modules 1 through 4. It will assess your understanding of LLMs, prompt engineering, AI applications, and problem-solving frameworks.

Exam Format

The exam will consist of multiple-choice questions, short-answer questions, and several scenario-based problems where you will be asked to write an effective prompt or design an AI-powered workflow to solve a given problem.

To begin the exam, you would typically click a "Start Exam" button here, which would load the questions and begin a timer.
