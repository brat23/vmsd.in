Module 2: Content Creation

Planning with AI

Generative AI can be a strategic partner in planning, optimizing, and distributing your content, helping you reach the right audience with the right message.

AI-Assisted Strategy

  • Topic Ideation: Use an LLM to brainstorm a list of content ideas based on your target audience and keywords.
  • SEO Optimization: Ask an AI to identify relevant keywords, suggest SEO-friendly titles, and write meta descriptions for your articles or videos.
  • Content Calendar Planning: Give the AI a list of your core content pillars and ask it to generate a content calendar for the next month, complete with topics, formats, and suggested publishing dates.
  • Content Repurposing: AI is incredibly efficient at repurposing content. You can give it a long-form blog post and ask it to create a Twitter thread, a LinkedIn post, a video script, and a series of email newsletter blurbs, all from the same source material.
