AI at Scale

Large enterprises are moving beyond small-scale experiments and are now strategically integrating AI into their core operations to drive massive transformation.

Example: Morgan Stanley

The financial services firm Morgan Stanley partnered with OpenAI to build a custom AI assistant for its financial advisors. The AI was trained on the company's vast library of internal market research and investment strategy documents. It allows the firm's 16,000 advisors to quickly find answers to complex financial questions and surface relevant insights for their clients, transforming how they access and utilize the firm's collective knowledge.
