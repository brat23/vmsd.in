Structuring Your Data Story

A good data story follows a clear narrative structure. AI can help you apply classic storytelling frameworks to your data to make it more engaging and memorable.

Common Frameworks

  • The Hero's Journey: Frame your data as a story of a challenge (the problem), a struggle (the analysis), and a resolution (the insight).
  • Problem-Solution: Clearly present the business problem your analysis is addressing and how your data-driven insight provides the solution.
  • Before-and-After: Show the state of things before a change, and then use data to demonstrate the impact or result of the change.

You can ask an LLM, "Structure the key findings from this report into a compelling story using the Problem-Solution framework."
