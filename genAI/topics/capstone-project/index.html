Capstone Project

Module 6: Final Assessment & Certification

Showcase Your Skills

The capstone project is your opportunity to apply everything you've learned by building one of the real-world AI projects from Module 5. You will choose one of the projects, build it, and submit it for evaluation.

Submission Requirements

You will be required to submit:

  • A link to your working application (e.g., on a public GitHub repository).
  • A short video demonstrating your project in action.
  • A written report describing your design process, the prompts you engineered, and what you learned from the experience.
