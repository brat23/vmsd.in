Module 2: Learning Acceleration

Making Learning Stick

Learning something new is only half the battle; you also have to remember it. AI can help you implement scientifically-backed techniques for improving long-term knowledge retention.

AI-Generated Quizzes

After reading a chapter or watching a lecture, ask an LLM to act as a quiz master. You can have it generate multiple-choice questions, short-answer questions, or even complex scenario-based problems to test your understanding of the material. This practice of active recall is one of the most effective ways to strengthen memory.

Spaced Repetition

Spaced Repetition is a technique where you review information at increasing intervals over time. You can use an AI to help you create flashcards from your notes and then build a study schedule based on the principles of spaced repetition, ensuring you review concepts at the optimal time to transfer them into long-term memory.
