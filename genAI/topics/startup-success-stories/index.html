Building the Future

Many of the most innovative companies are now "AI-first," meaning they are built from the ground up around a core generative AI capability.

Example: Jasper.ai

Jasper (formerly Jarvis) was one of the first major startups to build a business around providing a user-friendly interface for a powerful LLM (initially GPT-3). They focused on the marketing and copywriting niche, creating templates and workflows specifically for those users. Their success demonstrated the huge market for tools that make generative AI accessible and useful for specific business functions.
