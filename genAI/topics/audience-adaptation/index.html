Audience Adaptation

Module 3: Data Storytelling

Speaking Their Language

The same data story may need to be told in very different ways to different audiences. An AI can help you tailor your language, tone, and focus for specific stakeholders.

Tailoring Your Message

After you have a core data story, you can prompt an LLM to adapt it. For example:

  • "Rewrite this analysis for a senior executive audience. Focus on the high-level business implications and the recommended actions."
  • "Explain these findings to a technical audience. Include details about the methodology and the statistical significance of the results."
  • "Turn this report into a short, engaging presentation for the sales team."
