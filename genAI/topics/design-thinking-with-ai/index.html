Design Thinking with AI

Module 4: Innovation Methodologies

Human-Centered Innovation

Design Thinking is a human-centered approach to innovation. AI can be integrated into every step of this process to augment the abilities of the human designers.

AI in the Design Thinking Process

  • Empathize: Use AI to analyze user interviews, surveys, and feedback to quickly identify key pain points and needs.
  • Define: Synthesize the findings from the empathy phase with AI to craft a clear, data-driven problem statement.
  • Ideate: Use an LLM as a brainstorming partner to generate a wide range of potential solutions to the problem.
  • Prototype: Use AI to create mockups, write draft copy, or even generate code for a low-fidelity prototype.
  • Test: Analyze user feedback on your prototype with AI to identify common themes and areas for improvement.
